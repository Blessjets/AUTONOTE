WELCOME  TO KANYAMA COMMUNITY

"Kanyama is one of the biggest communities in Lusaka. The community has been known to be a great community of love, unity and perseverance despite  a few challenges that can easily turn into  crisis i.e floods and water born diseases during rain season"  More about Kanyama.

KANYAMA FIRST LEVEL HOSPITAL

Kanyama first level hospital  is the only public hospital in the area, following its upgrade from a clinic. However the upgrade was made to reduce the number of referrals to the University Teaching Hospital and Levy Mwanawasa Hospital
